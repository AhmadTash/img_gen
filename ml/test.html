General Information

If you complete all your courses in this semester successfully, you will start your internship in the next (spring semester of 2025-2026) semester.

  • Duration: 70 working days = 14 weeks = 1 semester (continuous)
  • Start Date: First day of spring semester (check academic calendar)
  • Document Submission: Face to face, at least one week before the start of the spring semester

Course Selection

  • IMUING402: If you will start your internship for the first time, you will select this course.
  • IMUING404: You will choose this course if you have been a full-time employee in a company for at least 6 months before starting your internship and have formally proven this.

Required Documents - For Companies in Turkey

1. Official Transcript

  • Select your course (IMUING402 or IMUING404) during course-registration week
  • Wait for approval
  • Check your transcript to confirm the course is approved
  • Get your official transcript from the student affairs office

2. Document No. 1 (Staj_Kabul_Formu)

Student Information Part:

  • Fill in your personal information
  • Faculty/School: Faculty of Engineering and Architecture
  • Department/Program: Software Engineering Department
  • Class and Type of Study: 4th year student and intern

Type Part:

  • Tick the Professional Practice (Internship) box

Institution Information Part:

  • Your company will fill out this part
  • Original signature from your company's engineer/manager required
  • Original stamp from your company required

3. Additional Documents

  • Document No. 2 (KM_FR_12_internship_commitment)
  • TC ID card photocopy OR passport photocopy

Additional Requirements - For Companies Outside Turkey

Submit all documents mentioned above (1, 2, and 3), plus:

  • Official insurance document from your company
Important: Please fill out all forms in your handwriting.

After Document Submission

Once you complete the first step, come to me and I will sign your documents.

During Your Internship

You will submit the report document face to face in the midterm-exam and final-exam weeks. The report document will be shared with you at the beginning of the spring semester.
